## Status

Accepted

## Context

Raw engagement events may become large and difficult to process directly.

## Decision

Decision systems should consume signals generated by the Insight Layer.

Direct access to raw events should not be the primary decision mechanism.

## Consequences

### Positive

- simpler decision models
- better performance
- improved scalability

### Negative

- signal generation must be maintained
